Weight Loss

Why weight losing or maintaining the reduced weight very hard?

We need to understand the very important term "HOMEOSTASIS" of our body. Homeostasis: The automatic tendency of our body to maintain a relatively constant internal environment. Examples: keep our body temperature at 98.6-degree Fahrenheit, keep our metabolic rate constant, Hormone production and concentration level maintenance, Blood glucose level and another nutrient level in the cell, Maintenance the serum oxygen levels and carbon dioxide level etc. Over emotions are also related to HOMEOSTASIS.

Our body weight is also controlled by Homeostasis. When we trying to change the bodyweight our HOMEOSTASIS work against our effort because it wants to maintain the same body weight. In weight losing our body and mind work against our will.We will definitely experience hunger, stress, sickness, mood swing, craving for surgery food etc... That's why most of the people give up their weight loss goals. If you want to reduce your body weight, you should make some strategy. Our qualified and experienced trainers will guide you through your successful weight loss journey
